SELL

We sell quality clothing, shoes and accessories. We sell the items
on your behalf.You get 50% of the sale price. Simple as that.
In store you’ll find a variety of designer, vintage and quality high
street brands.We carefully accept pieces based on
age, quality, style, brand, and season, as well
as what we see selling in store. As a general rule, if you wouldn’t
give it to a friend, we probably won’t be able to take it either!

CLOTHING CRITERIA

• Clothing must be freshly laundered, good quality, in great condition,
and match the current season. Check our stores page to see what season
your store is currently accepting.

• If you are dropping off in store we must be able to accept
a minimum of 5 items, or 10 if we are picking up from you.
So the more you put in your drop, the better!

• We only accept clothing, shoes, and accessories.

• We look for current or timeless styles and generally won’t
accept pieces older than 2 years, unless vintage. Check this list for some examples of brands we don't take.
